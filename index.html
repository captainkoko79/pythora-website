About Pythora

Pythora calculates six personal numbers from your birth date and today's date, then generates a reading uniquely tailored to you. This is not a generic horoscope — it's a precise numerological forecast built from your Life Path, Personal Year, Personal Month, Personal Day, Pinnacle, and Challenge numbers.

The system is based on Pythagorean numerology, the most widely practiced method worldwide, as taught by Hans Decoz. Each number is weighted by its temporal proximity to today, so your reading always reflects what's most relevant right now.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my reading calculated?

Pythora uses the Pythagorean method. It takes your birth date and reduces it alongside today's date to produce six personal numbers. These are weighted by how close they are to the present moment — your Personal Day carries the most influence (50%), followed by Month (25%), Year (15%), Pinnacle (5%), Challenge (3%), and Life Path (2%).

Why does my reading change when I refresh?

Refreshing requests a fresh AI-generated interpretation of your numbers. The underlying numbers stay the same for the day — only the prose changes. The app limits refreshes to 10 per day to prevent excessive generation costs.

Can I change my birth date?

Yes. Go to the Settings tab, tap the edit (pencil) icon on your profile card, enter your new date of birth, and tap Calculate. Your entire numerology profile will be recalculated instantly.

Does the app work offline?

Once today's reading has loaded, it is cached and available offline for the rest of the day. Generating a new reading or refreshing requires an internet connection.

What are master numbers?

In Pythagorean numerology, 11, 22, and 33 are considered "master numbers" and are not reduced to a single digit. Pythora recognizes and handles all three master numbers throughout your profile and daily readings.

Is the app free?

Yes, Pythora is free to download and use. The app is supported by advertising displayed within the daily reading screen.

How do I change the language?

Tap the Settings tab and select English or Tiếng Việt under the language section. Your next reading will be generated in the chosen language.

Privacy Policy

Pythora collects no personal information beyond your birth date, which is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers. It is used solely to calculate your numerology numbers.

The app uses a randomly generated anonymous identifier (UUID) to associate your reading requests with our backend. This ID contains no personally identifiable information.

Analytics: Pythora logs anonymised in-app events (e.g. "reading viewed", "language changed") to help us understand how the app is used. No names, emails, or device identifiers are collected.

Advertising: Pythora uses Google AdMob to display native advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised ads in your iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

If you have any questions about privacy, contact us at support@pythoraapp.com.
